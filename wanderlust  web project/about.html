😎About

Wanderlust is a UK-based, independent travel magazine established in 1993,
known for its on "talking the road travelled" through
content that highlights lesser-known destinations,
sustainable travel, unigue experiences and responsible tourism.
Published in both print and digital formats bi-monthly.
Wanderlust is the longest-running and largest-circulation travel magazine in the UK and Europe
